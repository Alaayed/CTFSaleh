#### Binomial Model 
Assumes some value will change to one of two possible values. For the approach, 4 values are needed.
- Initial value of the underlying asset
- An exercise price for the option, can be different from the underlying asset, assumption is that it expires in one period.
- Returns from the up-move and down move of the underlying asset over the period.
- The risk free rate over the period.
